How It Works

A forensic pipeline that goes beyond surface-level analysis to expose the artifacts left by AI voice synthesis.

1
Step 01 — Ingestion

Upload or Record Audio

Submit a clip by uploading a file or recording directly in-browser. DeepDetect accepts all major audio formats and automatically resamples to 16kHz mono — the optimal format for neural speech analysis. Clips between 2 and 13 seconds yield the highest accuracy.

WAVMP3FLACOGGM4A16 kHz mono
2
Step 02 — Signal Analysis

Deep Signal Decomposition

The raw audio is decomposed into amplitude waveforms and Mel spectrograms simultaneously. This dual representation captures both temporal dynamics and frequency characteristics — providing the model with a complete acoustic fingerprint of the speech signal.

WaveformMel SpectrogramMFCCPitchZCR
3
Step 03 — Neural Inference

Wav2Vec2 Classification

A fine-tuned Wav2Vec2 transformer processes the acoustic features. Pre-trained on 960 hours of speech data, it identifies the subtle artifacts left by text-to-speech engines and voice cloning systems — artifacts that are entirely invisible to the human ear.

Wav2Vec2TransformerSelf-SupervisedLibriSpeech
4
Step 04 — Verdict

Forensic Evidence Report

Results are presented with a probability score, risk classification, and a full evidence table breaking down each acoustic metric analysed. You get a clear Real or Fake verdict backed by explainable signal-level reasoning — not a black box decision.

Capabilities

Key Features

Built for researchers, journalists, and security professionals who need reliable audio deepfake detection.

Real-Time Detection

Record audio live from your microphone and receive a verdict within seconds. Analysis runs via a local API — no data leaves your machine.

Signal Visualisation

Every analysis renders an amplitude waveform and Mel spectrogram, letting you visually inspect the acoustic signature of any submitted audio clip.

Explainable Evidence

A detailed evidence table surfaces every acoustic metric analysed — pitch, spectral flatness, zero-crossing rate, and more — each with a per-metric verdict.

Risk Classification

Each verdict is tagged LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, or CRITICAL based on confidence thresholds. Know at a glance exactly how certain the model is.

Multi-Format Support

Accepts WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, and M4A. Automatic resampling ensures consistent analysis regardless of the input file's original sample rate.

Wav2Vec2 Backbone

Built on Meta's Wav2Vec2 — a self-supervised transformer pre-trained on 960 hours of LibriSpeech — fine-tuned specifically for audio deepfake detection.
